The Solstice Sentinels: Building the S'moregåsborg

Campaign Log: Seasonal Summer Entry. A sacred mission to construct the definitive structure of summertime delight.

The Sentinel's Decree: A Structure of Summertime Delight

The Sweet Adventures Club received a solemn decree from the Solstice Sentinels—a group of powerful artificers and guardians of perfect summer nights. The seasonal dessert must be the S'moregåsborg, a culinary structure so perfect it captures the essence of a campfire under the stars. This required our Master Wizards and Sorcerers to become architects, mastering three distinct layers of enchantment to achieve true harmony.

The quest began with the Foundation Layer: the golden, honey-sweet Honeycrumb (graham crust). This required a challenging Structural Integrity Check to ensure the crust could support the density of the rich Underdark Fondant (dark chocolate cheesecake). Too soft, and the entire edifice would collapse; too hard, and the magic would not bind. With the foundation set, the focus shifted to the core itself—a powerful, dark chocolate enchantment that provided the necessary depth and richness.

The Cloud Factory: Ascending to the Marshmallow Heights

The most unstable element was the final layer: the marshmallow fluff, or Sugarfluff. The Sentinels demanded a topping that was light, sweet, and perfectly stable—a cloud that would not dissipate or deflate. Our Sorcerers faced a difficult Aeration Check in the Hearth Forge (Headquarters), wrestling with sugar crystals and temperature control.

During the whisking phase, the Party encountered a sudden failure—a Chaos Event Roll caused the mixture to seize and turn grainy. The Wizards had to quickly cast an emergency Reconstitution Spell (precise application of heat and acid) to save the fluff, restoring it to a perfectly smooth, ethereal cloud. The final marshmallow layer of Sugarfluff was light, sweet, and ready to complete the legendary structure.

The Final Assembly: Completing the S'moregåsborg

With all three powerful components complete, the Party began the final Assembly Ritual. The Underdark Fondant was carefully set upon the Honeycrumb, and then crowned with the billowing Sugarfluff. This entire structure required a final, steady hand and a Balance Check (DC 19) to prevent the layers from mixing prematurely.

Success! The S'moregåsborg is complete: a single-serve, multi-layered architectural masterpiece that offers rich chocolate, crunchy graham, and sweet, airy marshmallow. This Epic Rarity artifact is available only during the Seasonal Summer Event. Adventurers, the structure is built! Claim your treasure in The Treasury (the online store) before the Solstice Sentinels call in the autumn air.
