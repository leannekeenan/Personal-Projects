Order Requests

An adventure to discover your favorite flavor starts here

We take great pride in our products and make them fresh to order to ensure the highest quality. Please follow the instructions to assist you in completing the order request form below.

Instructions

  • Order Request: The form you are completing is an Order Request. Upon receiving your submission, we will confirm your requested date and item availability and send a digital invoice via Quickbooks. Orders must be placed at least two (2) weeks (14 days) prior to ensure deliverability.
  • Order Confirmation: Your order is considered confirmed and enters our production schedule only after full payment is received.
  • Lead Time Requirement: To ensure the freshest ingredients and timely scheduling, full payment must be received a minimum of two (2) weeks (14 days) prior to your desired delivery date. Orders with less than two weeks' notice may be declined.

Delivery and Fees

We offer delivery within the greater Peninsula/Bay Area, from San Francisco to San Jose.

Delivery fees are calculated based on the distance from our bakery. All prices are subject to tax.
