Menu

An adventure to discover your favorite flavor starts here

At Sweet Adventures Club, we believe that every dessert should be a journey of flavor and delight. Our menu is crafted with the finest ingredients and a touch of magic to create an unforgettable experience. Whether you're craving something classic or adventurous, we have a treat that will satisfy your sweet tooth and ignite your imagination.

Signature Flavors
Vanilla Veil A delight of impossible velvety smoothness - its essence is woven with Madagascar Bourbon Vanilla, resting on a gilded foundation of honey graham cracker. This magical pairing is a flavor so pure it defies the laws of gravity.
Cosmic Chocolate A decadence so intensely fudgy it bends starlight. Using Dutch Cocoa for a richly indulgent experience atop a crushed field of Oreo, and its surface dusted with cosmic sprinkles— it's a burst of pure, stellar bliss.
Cinnasphere A warm, swirling vortex of brown sugar and cinnamon is bound within a velvet-smooth core. This rich, spiced confection rests on a buttery graham gracker crust and topped with a sweet vanilla icing—an ancient flavor etched in the sands of time.
Peanut Butter Portal This dense peanut butter threshold is channeled through a ribbon of semi-sweet chocolate aloft a platform of Oreo. A yawning flavor gateway that packs a taste so intense, it will instantly transport your mind to your own personal interdimentional doorway.
Cookies & Clouds An impossibly soft, velvet cloud scattered with rich Oreo fragments—the sacred stardust of the pastry realm. It rests upon a deep cocoa-crumb foundation of Oreo. This light taste grants weightless escape to the sweet heavens.
Seasonal specials
Fall - Spice Gourdmand

A rare blend of autumn spices and real pumpkin puree, masterfully merged into a rich, decadent filling rests upon a spiced crust of Amaretti gingersnap and honey graham cracker. Careful - to seek this flavor so potent may become the sole purpose of your culinary quest!

Winter - Aethereal Apple

A legendary core of caramel and apple with warm spices, encased in the soft embrase of vanilla. This precious confection rests on a buttery, graham cracker trove—a powerful treat that promises a golden reward with every bite.

Spring - Strawberry Starlight

A light, vanilla core topped with a shimmering, ruby-red strawberry jam, grounded in a lemon zest honey-graham crust, instantly transporting you to a blossoming, sun-drenched garden where all life begins anew.

Summer - S'moregåsborg

An overflowing smörgåsbord of molten, dark chocolate and fire-toasted marshmallow, capturing the essence of sacred bonfire magic. This alchemic feast rests on a buttery, graham platform, unleashing a potent, sweet spell of pure summer nostalgia.
