How to Play

  1. Roll for initiative to determine who will choose a card in the river first.

  2. Find a number of "Right Path" cards as decided by the DM.

    • Provides added difficulty to your dungeon (it’s recommended you start with finding three and add from there).

  3. Choose cards in the river to progress through the dungeon. Beware of traps and monsters!

    • See Card Types for Card Effects.

  4. The DM will determine what happens to the party in the dungeon based on the card drawn in the river (check the card types for details).

  5. All three cards in the river must be played before the DM draws a new river.

  6. Once all cards in the river are shown and the playthrough is complete, a new river will populate to restart the next round. How long will it take your party to escape?

Card Types

  • Right Path

    Finding this card means you are one step closer to escaping the dungeon!

  • Dead End

    You and your party have run into a dead end; you will have to find another way to go.

  • Monster

    Your party runs the risk of encountering a monster! Can your party make it out alive?

    * When a "Monster Card" is found twice, it triggers an encounter.

  • Treasure

    Your party finds something awesome!

  • Trap

    Your party has to escape one of the many booby traps in the dungeon! Will you survive?

Action Types

  • Forge a Path

    Add 2 "Right Path" Cards to deck

  • Scout Ahead

    Add top card of the deck to the river

  • It's Elementary

    Draw three additional cards, choose one to move to the bottom of the deck

  • Careful Consideration:

    Add two random cards in the river

  • Boost Morale

    Remove a dead end card from the deck
